About Us

TTAF Lighting, which was established in 2003 to realize special product designs for its customers in Led Lighting Applications, serves in its 6000m2 Istanbul facility.

Supporting domestic production, TTAF Lighting manufactures indoor and outdoor lighting products and accessories for the basic needs of different sectors with its experience in the sector, dynamic staff and joint work with the world's most important manufacturers.

TTAF Lighting believes in the importance of innovation and R&D for sustainable success in the electricity and electronics sector, has a multi-disciplinary structure that closely follows the developments in lighting solutions and reflects the studies in this direction to its products within the framework of international standards.
